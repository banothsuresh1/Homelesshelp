About Us

Hi, I am Banoth Suresh, an M.Tech student at VNR VJIET. I work part-time as a delivery boy for Swiggy. During my night shifts, I noticed many people sleeping on the roadside without blankets or food. Their plight moved me, and I decided to take action.

I created this initiative to provide warmth, food, and hope to those in need. With your support, we can make a real difference and bring smiles to many faces. Let’s stand together and help the homeless.

  • Our Goal: To distribute essential items like blankets and food to those who need them most.
  • Our Promise: Every contribution goes directly toward helping the homeless.
  • Our Belief: Together, we can change lives and build a better community.
